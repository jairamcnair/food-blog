Jump to Recipe Introduction

This creamy chicken salad, with a bit of a kick, is foolproof, affordable, and crave-worthy. Chicken salad tastes better the longer it sits, making it great for a weekly lunch meal prep, not to mention it's portable, protein packed, and versatile. Make it a low-carb option with a lettuce wrap or a high-card option with bread.

The Ingredients

Chicken is the star of the show. Use any chicken you have. This recipe is great for leftover chicken you need to use up.

Mayonnaise acts as a creamy binder and a neutral flavored base.

Diced pickled jalapenos w/ juice will be the main flavor and add texture and crunch.

Salt brings out the other flavors.

Spices: Pepper, onion powder, and garlic powder add extra flavor and dimension.

Substitutes & Additives

Greek yogurt: You can substitute the mayo with Greek yogurt for less fat and more protein, or do half and half. The yogurt is a bit more bitter.

Fresh cilantro: Fresh cilantro leaves tastes great with this recipe and would add some color and also be a nice garnish.

Health Benefits

Chicken salad can be a healthy, balanced meal when prepared thoughtfully. For optimal health, use grilled or baked chicken instead of fried; use Greek yogurt instead of mayo (or do half and half); add some herbs and/or veggies (like onion, cilantro); and if you need to be mindful of carbs, use low-carb bread, lettuce wraps, or use it as a veggie dip for celery, bell peppers, and more.

Benefits

Chicken: B vitamins (especially B6 and niacin) for energy metabolism

High protein + fiber (from veggies) helps stabilize blood sugar levels

Directions

Step 1:

Bring a large pot to a boil, add in:

2 lb. chicken breast

Boil until done.

Tip: Cut the chicken small for faster cooking. The smaller the chicken, the quicker it'll cook.

Step 2:

Add the chicken to a blender and pulse until it is as fine as you want it. Dump the chicken in a bowl and re-blend the big pieces that missed the blade.

Step 3:

Dump the minced chicken out into a bowl and add:

0.333 cups mayo

0.5 diced pickled jalapenos

0.25 cups pickled jalapeno juice

0.75 tsp salt

0.75 tsp pepper

0.25 tsp garlic powder

0.125 tsp onion powder

Mix until well combined. Season more to taste.

I do everything to taste. These measurements are just a guide. Consider adding fresh herbs if you have any. I love adding fresh minced cilantro, but dill or parsley would be good as well.
