Jump to Recipe Introduction

There's nothing better than a fresh, crisp, cold salad on a summer evening. Never over-complicate a salad. All you need is a few ingredients, some olive oil, some aromatics, a vinegar, and some spices to create a great salad and dressing.

The Ingredients

Cucumber is crunchy, hydrating, low-carb, low-calorie, and full of fiber and antioxidants, making it perfect for a nutritious snack or side dish.

Tomato are also rich in antioxidants, have immunity-boosting properties, and have fiber to help regulate blood sugar and aid in digestion.

Red wine vinegar will add a tangy flavor, with very subtle hints of red wine. It will help emulsify the dressing and aid in digestion.

*Regular* olive oil will be the base of the salad dressing, adding a mild, yet neutral olive oil flavor.

Honey will be used to sweeten the dressing.

Salt will bring out the other flavors.

Spices: Black pepper and oregano are the main flavors, making the dressing earthy and versatile.

Lemon juice will add a fresh, bright flavor.

Minced garlic cloves will add a hint of garlic.

Fresh dill adds a fresh, bright flavor that pairs well with the lemon, and gives an aromatic appeal.

Substitutes & Additives

White wine vinegar, balsamic vinegar, or, ACV can be used instead of red wine vinegar.

Fresh parsley can be used instead of dill for a milder flavor.

Health Benefits

Cucumber-tomato salad is simple, refreshing, and surprisingly nutrient-dense. When eaten fresh, it combines the hydration and fiber of cucumber with the antioxidants and vitamins of tomato, giving several health benefits:

Hydration: Cucumbers are around 95% water, and tomatoes are around 94% water. Together, they'll help maintain fluid balance and support kidney function.

Antioxidants: Tomatoes have lycopene, vitamin C, beta-carotene, which support heart health, and may reduce inflammation and oxidative stress. Cucumbers contain flavonoids and tannins, which help protect cells and reduce inflammation.

Directions

Step 1:

In 2+ cup measuring pitcher, mix:

3 TBSP red wine vinegar

2 TBSP *regular* olive oil

1 TBSP honey

1 tsp salt

0.5 tsp black pepper

0.5 tsp oregano

1 TBSP lemon juice

1 small minced garlic cloves

2 TBSP chopped dill, fresh

Whisk well and set aside.

Step 2:

Chop:

1 english cucumber

4 roma tomatoes

Put them in a large bowl

Step 3:

Pour on the dressing and stir the salad to coat the vegetables.

Serve and enjoy!
