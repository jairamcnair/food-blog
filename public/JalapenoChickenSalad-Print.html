Dairy & Eggs:

Meat:

chicken breast, 2 lb.

Use any chicken you have.

Directions

Step 1:

Bring a large pot to a boil, add in:

2 lb. chicken breast

Boil until done.

Tip: Cut the chicken small for faster cooking. The smaller the chicken, the quicker it'll cook.

Step 2:

Add the chicken to a blender and pulse until it is as fine as you want it. Dump the chicken in a bowl and re-blend the big pieces that missed the blade.

Step 3:

Dump the minced chicken out into a bowl and add:

0.333 cups mayo

0.5 diced pickled jalapenos

0.25 cups pickled jalapeno juice

0.75 tsp salt

0.75 tsp pepper

0.25 tsp garlic powder

0.125 tsp onion powder

Mix until well combined. Season more to taste.

I do everything to taste. These measurements are just a guide. Consider adding fresh herbs if you have any. I love adding fresh minced cilantro, but dill or parsley would be good as well.
