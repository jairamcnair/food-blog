Directions

Step 1:

Start pre-heating your waffle maker.

Step 2:

In a medium or large glass measuring pitcher, add:

0.5 cups all-purpose flour

1 TBSP brown sugar

0.5 tsp baking powder

0.125 tsp salt (original measurement - 1/8 tsp)

Whisk until well combined.

Step 3:

Now add the wet ingredients:

1.5 TBSP *regular* olive oil

1 whole egg(s)

0.333 cups whole milk (original measurement - 1/3 cup)

Whisk until combined, but don't over mix the batter.

Step 4:

Pour half the batter into the 8-inch waffle maker. Cook until done. Repeat with the remaining batter.

Serve and enjoy!
