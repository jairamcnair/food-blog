Directions

Step 1:

In a wide-based bowl, whisk:

0.25 cups whole milk

1 whole egg

1 TBSP white sugar

0.25 tsp vanilla extract

0.25 tsp ground Saigon cinnamon

Whisk until well combined. This is the custard.

Step 2:

In another small bowl, mix:

1 TBSP white sugar

0.25 tsp ground Saigon cinnamon

This is the optional cinnamon-sugar coating for you to coat the toast in after you cook the bread.

Step 3:

Begin heating a non-stick pan over medium heat. Cut your bread into 1.5-inch by 1-inch (by however long) sticks or leave the slices whole.

Drench the slices in the milk-mixture and put them in the skillet. Cook each side until golden brown. Now (optional), coat each stick or slice in the cinnamon sugar coating you made.

Serve and enjoy! Top with your favorite toppings or eat it how it is.
